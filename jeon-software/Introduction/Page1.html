No Talking Allowed
We ask that you do not talk. If you have a question, please raise your hand, and the experimenter will approach you and answer your question in private.
Introduction

This is an experiment in the economics of abstract decision making. Georgia State University has provided funds for conducting this research. This study is expected to take no more than one hour to complete. You will receive $3 for participating in this study.

The experiment consists of two parts and a survey. You will earn an additional amount based on decisions that you and other participants make. At the end of the experiment, you will randomly select one part, and earnings from that part will be used to calculate your final earnings. Your final earnings will be paid to you in cash in private at the end of the experiment.

If you have any questions, or need assistance of any kind, please raise your hand and wait for the experimenter.

Please click Start to begin the experiment.
