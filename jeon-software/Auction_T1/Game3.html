Instructions

In this task, you are paired with a computer opponent who makes a random bid between $0.00 and $10.00 in increments of $0.01.

In summary,
o If you WIN:

o If you TIE, the winner will be selected at random:
     ‣ 50% chance that you WIN
     ‣ 50% change that you LOSE

Your bidding decision
